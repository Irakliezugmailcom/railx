Executive Summary

We ask the Board to approve a six-month build of RailX, an AI-powered website that matches empty rail capacity to cargo on the Poland–Ukraine corridor, at a fixed price between €20,000 and €28,000 (hard ceiling €35,000). Three vetted developers will submit Best & Final Offers within five days of approval. Operating cost after launch is approximately €800/month, covered by two operator subscribers. Year 1 base revenue projection: €294,000.

§1 Purpose

This memorandum requests Board approval of (a) the product scope, (b) the budget envelope, (c) the six-month timeline with four milestone payments, and (d) the procurement process at §10.

§2 Background

Block train operators on the Poland–Ukraine corridor lose €8,000 to €20,000 per empty backhaul because they have no efficient way to find return cargo. Cargo owners overpay long-term contracts because no spot market exists. Today both sides find each other through phone calls and WhatsApp groups. RailX is first to market on this corridor.

§3 The product

3.1 What it is

A website at railx.eu. Works on any browser, computer or phone. Not a mobile application. Not a chatbot. The AI assistant — called Rex — is the product.

3.2 What users see

ViewFunction
Rex chatThe main view. User types a request; Rex answers, asks follow-up questions, finds matches, prepares paperwork.
Listings feedA scrollable, Facebook-style list of trains and cargo. Filter by route, date, wagon type. Click any post to message the poster.
MessengerPrivate chat between the two parties; opens after the €150 commitment fee is paid.

3.3 How users interact — two paths

Path A · Ask Rex

Cargo owner types in Ukrainian:

"I need 20 tonnes of grain from Lviv to Hamburg next Tuesday. Standard gauge wagons."

Rex answers in Ukrainian. Asks two clarifying questions. Shows three matching trains with prices.

Path B · Browse the feed

Same user scrolls the listings feed:

Filters: Lviv → Hamburg / Tuesday / standard gauge → 3 matching posts

User clicks one. Rex drafts a polite opening message in Ukrainian.

Both paths converge: pay €150 → carrier's contact appears → private messenger opens → 48-hour response guarantee runs. If the carrier is silent for 48 hours, the €150 is refunded.

3.4 Languages

Rex auto-detects and converses in English, Polish, and Ukrainian.

3.5 Rex performs six jobs

#JobWhat it does
1ListingsCarriers create capacity posts via conversation.
2DiscoveryCargo owners receive ranked matches with rationale.
3CommitmentHandles €150 payment, 48-hour guarantee, contact disclosure.
4MessengerDrafts notes, summarises threads, escalates to WhatsApp.
5VerificationChecks Polish UTK and Ukrainian rail authority licenses.
6IntelligencePost-launch · market trends · €2,500/month subscription.
